# Initial Prototype

Template and rubric for the Initial Prototype assignment in the Design of AT program.

## Before You Build

Students were expected to document the following **before** physically building:

- **Design Objectives** — clear statement of what the prototype should accomplish
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** — list of all materials and components needed
- **Technical Drawing** — dimensioned sketch or CAD drawing of the planned design

## Testing Plan and Results

After building, students documented:

- **Testing Plan** — how the prototype would be tested against design objectives
- **Test Results** — what actually happened during testing

## Prototype Conclusions

- Summary of what was learned from the initial prototype
- Key issues to address in the next iteration
- Decisions for the next design cycle
