# Final Prototype

Template for the Final Prototype documentation in the Design of AT program.

## Required Documentation Sections

- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** — complete list of all materials and components
- **Build Instructions** — step-by-step guide for recreating the prototype
- **Test Plan** — methodology for evaluating the final prototype
- **Test Results** — outcomes of testing against design objectives
- **Future Improvements** — what would be changed or enhanced with more time
- **Scalability** — how the design could be produced in quantity or adapted for similar users
- **Design Process Reflection** — what was learned through the full design process, from co-designer interviews to final prototype
