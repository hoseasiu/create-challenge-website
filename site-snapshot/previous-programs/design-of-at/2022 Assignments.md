# 2022 Assignments

The 2022 Design of AT course used a **milk-themed naming system** for assignment milestones ("Gates"). Each Gate linked to a Google Drive template.

## Assignment Gates (in order)

1. **Cheese Gate**
2. **Yogurt Gate**
3. **Whey Gate**
4. **Condensed Gate**
5. **Heavy Cream Gate**
6. **Light Cream Gate**

Each gate had a specific due date and linked to a Google Drive template document.

> *Note: The milk-themed names are presentation/milestone checkpoints, not product categories. The templates linked from each gate structured the required documentation for that stage.*
