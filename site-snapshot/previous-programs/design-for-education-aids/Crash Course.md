# Design for Accessible Educational Aids — Crash Course

Students use **computer-aided design (CAD)** to create a unique **3D-printed tactile educational tool** that can help teach STEM concepts to students who are blind, have learning disabilities, or just enjoy learning with physical objects. The puzzle boxes are produced and donated to a local special education partner and featured on the site for others to use.

## Course Details

- **Format:** Virtual, offered on Saturdays in March and April 2021
- **Program:** BWSI Crash Course program
- **Audience:** 9th and 10th graders interested in engineering who lack access to such programs in their schools

## Note on Documentation

Documentation for student projects is **incomplete** for this course because public project documentation was a post-course pilot (the first time the program tried gathering work for an online repository, assembled after the course had ended).
