Assignment Notes

All assignments and submissions are due 23:59:59 Anywhere on Earth on the day indicated. The assignment is not late until it is past the date indicated everywhere on the planet.


You may always submit assignments early. If the submission form is not yet available, please contact staff.


  • We recommend starting to find a co-designer as soon as possible.
  • The Maker Skills section of the course involves selecting and completing two relevant modules from that section. We recommend starting on that section as soon as you have a sense of what type of skills your project may require.
