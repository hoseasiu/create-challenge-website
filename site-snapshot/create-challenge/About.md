# About

> **Snapshot note:** Full page content was captured on 2026-05-11 but is not reproduced here. Visit [https://beaver-works-assistive-tech.mit.edu/create-challenge/about](https://beaver-works-assistive-tech.mit.edu/create-challenge/about) for the live page.

Overview of the CRE[AT]E Challenge — a year-round, free assistive technology design challenge for high school students. Teams of 2–5 students partner with a "co-designer" (a person with a disability) to design and build an assistive device. Run by MIT Beaver Works.
